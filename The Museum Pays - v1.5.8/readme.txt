The Museum Pays - Get Paid Donating to the Museum [1.5.8]

This mod adds a new way to get paid in the valley and that's by your own donations to the museums, for every 10 donations to the museum you'll now receive a letter the next day with thanking you 1000/5000/10000 gold.
